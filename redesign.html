Redesign Of Website

This project was the first group project of the semester. Each group was given an already existing website, that wasn’t user-friendly and where the design didn’t work.

My group got a james bond museum website, which had so much content, that you didn’t knew where to start. My main part of the group work, was the user test, and then I also helped out with the design of the website.

User / Expert Test & Research

We made a 5-second test and an expert test, to figure out what we had to do to make the website user-friendly and recognizable for the user.

we also did a lot of research on the owner of the museum, to get an image of who he was as a person, his identity and as wells as his museum's identity. This made it a lot easier to create a website that fitted his and his museum's personality.
