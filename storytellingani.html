Storytelling & Character Design

This project was an Individual project. It consisted of a spritesheet animation and animation document. We should each choose a style we wanted to make our character and its environment in.

The style I chose was the Year walk style. I liked the way the style played with a layered landscape to create an almost 3D effect. I wanted my spritesheet animation to have the same papercut feel and 3D effect as the Year walk style.
